Welcome to Binh's
@ Mississauga

Binh's Nail and Spa is a one-stop shop for artistic nail designs, eyelash extensions, facials, waxing and related services.

Our expertise and experiences in the industry ensure your satisfaction. We focus on customers' experiences, wants, and needs. We cater our services to customers' lifestyles to maximize their comforts. Our prices will always be upfront and transparent to ensure our best customer service and avoid customers' unpleasant surprises.

Our team of nail technicians are not only very experienced, but also very professional in customer service to reinforce our best care to our customers.

At Binh's Nail and Spa, we carry a wide range of nail care and polishing products, from regular polish, shellac, to acrylic powder, dip powder, and BiO-Gel for best protection and long-lasting beauty of nails. We take our nail polishing service to the next level with various artistic designs of Chrome Effects, Glitter, Blooming Gel, French Whites, Ombré White, Color Ombré, Beads & Accents, Cat-eye, 3D, Art design by hand, Crystal Diamonds, and Swarovski Stones.

Start your nail-care journey with us today, let us pamper you and make you beautiful!
